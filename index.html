Our philosophy is simple. We invest in people and ideas. Oftentimes they’re inextricably linked, however, not in all cases. We work closely with those that want to make a change and build businesses around ideas that have the potential enact it.

We don’t consider our investments ‘deals’ and we don’t refer to our companies as a ‘portfolio’. Much like our founders who forge new paths and create new industries. We’re carving our own approach to venture.
