We're Elbow
Our philosophy is simple. We invest in two things, people and ideas. We believe the people we work with can take their ideas from back of the envelope concepts to raise additional capital through the demonstration of user-centred design, key user acquisition, and sound growth strategies and metrics.
We don’t consider our investments deals, or transactions, we talk about our elbow family. A growing ecosystem of people and independent thought centred around wanting to do more and never being complacent.
